DNHS Branch Sign Ups

Session 3: 7/3/2021 - 8/7/2021

Coding for Kidz is a club that was created in Del Norte High School.
The main goal of this club is to teach kids basic coding and introduce them to the STEM field.
This club will meet every Saturday. During this situation, we plan to hold our classes through Zoom or Google Meet.
The classes will be held on Saturdays (2pm-3pm) starting on July 3, 2021.
The first meeting (July 26) will be a parent information meeting and classes will start the following Saturday.
Right now, we teach Scratch and Python. We plan to create a web development class in the future if there is enough interest.
More information is on the form.
 
This signup is for the Del Norte High School Branch.
If you are not part of the DNHS branch,
contact a Coding4Kidz branch in your region.
