Coding4Kidz

Sessions: ~ 2 months

Coding for Kidz is a club that was created in Del Norte High School.
The main goal of this club is to teach kids basic coding and introduce them to the STEM field.
Class times change between all the branches.
Sessions will last about 2 months but can vary between branches.
Main Syllabus for all classes: Scratch, Python, Java. Other branches can teach more than this.
More information is on the form.
 
If you are not part of the DNHS branch,
contact a Coding4Kidz branch in your region.
